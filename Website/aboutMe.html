My Story

Hey! I'm Logan Selley! Born and raised in Spokane Washington, but I'm finishing my degree in Geography here at the University of Washington and plan on staying in Seattle after graduation! I'm now 23 and my hobbies mostly revolve around technology whether that's custom computers or peripherials to games, but I also really enjoy politics, anime and japanese culture! I'm also a musician, and I spent a lot of my time here at university playing trombone for the Husky Marching Band and making some truly unforgettable memories. Academically I'm really interested in the ways that spatiality and location effect a lot of problems in humanistic geography and with the continued rise of GIS technology it's an exciting time to be a geographer. With a concentration in Data Science and a minor in Informatics, I'm also uniquely qualified when it comes to managing, interpreting and organizing data, especially when it's spatial. As I'm graduating soon I am actively looking for new employment opportunities to take me into the next section of my life so please don't hesitate to contact me!
